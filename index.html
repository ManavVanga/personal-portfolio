About Me

I am a Research Associate at Bioprinting Laboratories Inc., working on 3D bioprinting platforms, human organoid culture, and perfusion-based assays for predictive drug discovery and NAMs-aligned research.

My expertise lies in hydrogel optimization, spheroid/organoid culture, and high-throughput assay development. I work closely on NIH-funded projects to advance standardized organoid models for safety assessment.

Passionate about bridging biotech innovation with translational impact, I focus on reproducibility, scalability, and human relevance in drug testing.
